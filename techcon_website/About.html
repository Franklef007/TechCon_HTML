History of TechCon

Since its founding in 2010, TechCon has grown to become one of the world's most prestigious technology conferences. From its humble beginnings as a tiny assembly of computer enthusiasts, it has grown to draw thousands of people, as well as innovators and industry executives.

Mission

Our goal is to encourage professionals in technology to work together and innovate. TechCon seeks to bring together professionals from diverse domains to exchange insights and steer the trajectory of technology.
